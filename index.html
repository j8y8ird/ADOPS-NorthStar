Section 03: The Evolution

Delivery Coordination → Operations → Intelligence

Three stages, each building on the last. The work happening now creates the foundation for what comes next, a function that increasingly removes overhead so ADLs can focus on the signals and decisions that matter.

NOW
H2 2026
Establish Consistency & Visibility
ADLs apply core metrics, conduct active backlog health reviews, and connect sprint-level output to epic progress through observation, experience, and close partnership with Product and Engineering.
Delivery Metrics Story Standards Backlog Reviews Epic Tracking
NEXT
Move from Reporting to Decision Support
ADOPS develops shared backlog health indicators and readiness signals. Delivery intelligence shifts from status reporting to surfacing team-level risks, flow patterns, and quality signals earlier in the delivery cycle.
Readiness Signals Flow Intelligence Capacity Trends Quality Patterns
FUTURE
Enable Intelligent Delivery Systems
Tooling and automation eliminate administrative drag, reducing the noise ADLs have to search through so they can focus on the signals that matter and make deliberate decisions about what to surface, react to, or guide.
Predictive Insights Automated Signals Epic-to-Delivery Alignment AI-Assisted Readiness
